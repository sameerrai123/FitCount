Beginner Level

Diet Plan:

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with fruits
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken with brown rice
  • Dinner: Steamed vegetables with tofu
  • Snacks: Nuts, fruits, Greek yogurt

Workout Plan:

  • Day 1: Stretch + Light Cardio
  • Day 2: Squats, Push-ups, Planks
  • Day 3: Yoga/Rest

Intermediate Level

Diet Plan:

  • Breakfast: Protein smoothie + eggs
  • Lunch: Peanut , lean meat or soya chunks
  • Dinner: Grilled fish or paneer with vegetables
  • Snacks: Boiled eggs, roasted chickpeas, Greek yogurt

Workout Plan:

  • Day 1: Chest and Shoulder (3 sets till failure)
  • Day 2: Back And Rear delt (3 sets till failure)
  • Day 3: Arms and Forearm (3 sets till failure)
  • Day 4: Legs (4 x till failure)
  • Day 5: Chest , Shoulder and Tricep (3 sets till failure)
  • Day 6: Back , Rear delt and Bicep (3 sets till failure)
  • Abs (3 sets till failure) Every alternate day

Advanced Level

Diet Plan:

  • High protein + complex carbs (brown rice, oats, sweet potatoes)
  • Protein sources: Eggs, fish, paneer, soya, chicken breast
  • Supplements: Whey, BCAAs, Creatine

Workout Plan:

  • Day 1: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps) (4 x 12)
  • Day 2: Pull (Back, Biceps) (4 x 12)
  • Day 3: Legs (Strength) (4 x 12)
  • Day 4: Arms (Form) (4 x 12)
  • Day 5: 30 min Cardio & Core Blast
  • Day 6: Full Body Conditioning
